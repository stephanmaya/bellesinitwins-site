Who We Are

We’re Stephan and Adrienne Bellesini — siblings, partners-in-crime, and proof that two heads really are better than one (especially when they’re both sleep-deprived and running on espresso). Between us, we’ve done just about everything that makes imaginary worlds feel real: Stephan animates characters until they breathe, blink, and bicker like old friends, while Adrienne makes sure every world they live in has the scuffs, scratches, and fingerprints of a life well-worn.

We grew up in the age of paper and pencils, but now we wrangle polygons, shaders, and particle systems like second nature. One of us can code his way out of a production problem, the other can texture a spoon so convincing you’ll swear you just licked soup off it. Together, we’re the whole pipeline disguised as two people — animators, modelers, storytellers, problem-solvers, and occasional composers when no one is listening.

We believe a good shot is like a good story: alive, appealing, and just a little unpredictable. If you’re looking for a duo who can carry projects from the first scribble to the final frame — with craft, wit, and just enough sibling banter to keep things lively — well, you’ve found us.
